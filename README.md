# 📊 D2C Customer Churn Prediction Dashboard

## 🔗 Live Demo
👉 [Click here to try the live app](https://d2c-churn-prediction-dashboard-bmecsstwq9dpjgahrtvuxr.streamlit.app/)
## 🏢 Business Problem
D2C brands lose customers silently every month.
This tool predicts WHO is about to leave BEFORE they do.

## 💰 Business Impact
- Identified £111,714 in Revenue at Risk
- 83.9% model accuracy
- Catches 90% of churners before they leave

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
Python, XGBoost, SHAP, Streamlit, Pandas
